About Me

Here's a little bit about me and my role in the data science community.

  • Network Engineering Background

    Before I got into data science and AI I worked as a network engineer. My duties included managing and building networks as well as troubleshooting data center issues.

  • Mathematic Approach to Data

    I really enjoy the mathematic aspect of Data Science. To me it is a real pleasure to think about parameters of different modeling algorithms and what systems can be used to most effectively answer tough questions.

  • Learned to Code to Automate Networks

    I started writing code when I worked for the government. The task of managing thousands of network devices that would break frequently was overwhelming, so to manage the huge task of network management, I automated many everyday network admin tasks.

  • Blogging for Tech Community

    I started my blog, The Journey Blog, to help like-minded people to feel less alone on the journey to keep up with the ever-changing tech industry and educate others on helpful insights I have found along the way.

  • I Love Deep Learning

    I am very passionate about all things deep learning. I love reading white papers and understanding the latest developments in AI research. My dream is to work on a computer vision lab.

  • Underfitted Social Club DAO

    I am part of a DAO (a decentralized autonomous organization on web 3.0) called the Underfitted Social Club. The purpose of the DAO is to make Machine Learning more accessible and easier to learn for the community of AI students.
